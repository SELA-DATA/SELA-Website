Timescribe

SELA is dedicated to streamlining the complex process of school timetabling and providing user-friendly dashboards for educational institutions. By leveraging higher secondary school data, SELA offers an innovative solution to optimize schedules and enhance operational efficiency. Our system is designed to alleviate the burdens associated with manual timetable creation, enabling schools to focus more on delivering quality education.

Timescribe and
Efficient Timetable Creation

SELA's platform utilizes advanced algorithms to analyze higher secondary school data and generate optimized timetables tailored to each institution's unique needs. This eliminates the tedious task of manually arranging classes, teachers, and resources, saving time and reducing errors.

With SELA, schools can create balanced schedules that maximize classroom utilization and minimize conflicts, ensuring smooth operations throughout the academic year.
